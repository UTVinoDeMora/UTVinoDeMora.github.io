About Us

Who we are?

Unión Tisaleña, founded in 2017 in Tisaleo, is an association of volunteers supported by Childfund. They initially focused on raising guinea pigs, but later diversified their efforts into wine and blackberry jam production. Their unity and dedication have transformed the local economy and improved community well-being.

VALUES

What are our values?

Teamwork

The phrase “working together” emphasizes the importance of collaboration and cooperation among team members to achieve common goals.

Experience

Our enterprise is built on a strong sense of responsibility, with everyone taking an active role in achieving common goals for the enterprise’s well-being.

Commitment

Working towards “common objectives” implies a high level of commitment and dedication to achieve the established goals.

Perseverance

The idea of having “scored in experience” suggests a continuous effort and an ability to overcome challenges over time.

Responsibility

Taking an active role in the work and in the achievement of common goals denotes a strong sense of responsibility.

Solidarity

Cooperation and joint work reflect a spirit of solidarity and mutual support.
